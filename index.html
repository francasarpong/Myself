About Nana

Nana is a positive person. She is energetic, full of hope about the future and desires to become an inspiration to young people, especially young women. In the next few years, Nana hopes to become a web developer. She is working hard towards her dream. Working in the IT industry comes with many benefits, including: the freedom or independence linked with its job nature, it offers freelancing opportunities, enhances creativity, provides quality training options, allows everyday learning and promotes career growth. Nana hopes her persistent demeanour about life drives her to make her dreams a reality. She is also business-oriented and hopes that IT will offer her the required skills to kickstart a career in IT. This page was created to show her learning skills in Web Development so far. Her hope is that you get inspired to do more for yourself and society. Let us keep learning and improving ourselves!
